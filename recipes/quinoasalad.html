Quinoa Salad

Description

Quinoa Salad is a healthy and delicious option. The quinoa is mixed with fresh vegetables, aromatic herbs, and a light vinaigrette. It's perfect for a nourishing lunch or as a side dish for a main meal.

Ingredients

  • Quinoa
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Feta Cheese
  • Parsley
  • Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  • Cook the Quinoa: Rinse the quinoa under cold water. In a pot, bring water to a boil, add quinoa, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 15 minutes. Once cooked, fluff the quinoa with a fork and let it cool.

  • Prepare the Vegetables: Chop the cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and red onion into small pieces. Crumble the feta cheese and finely chop the parsley.

  • Make the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

  • Combine Ingredients: In a large bowl, mix the cooled quinoa with the vegetables, feta cheese, and parsley. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.

  • Serve: Serve the quinoa salad chilled or at room temperature. It's great as a light lunch or a side dish.
